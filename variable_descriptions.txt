1. `anti_pluralism` - Measures opposition to democratic pluralism. High values = party rejects opposition                       legitimacy, media freedom, or civil society. 

2. `populism`- Captures “people vs. elite” narratives. High values = party blames elites,                                oversimplifies issues, promotes itself as sole voice of people. 

3. `anti_elitism` - Component of populism. High values = distrust of elites, blame them for national         issues. 

4. `people_centrism`- Component of populism. High values = party believes only it represents the true                          will of the people.

5. `political_pluralism` - Measures support for political competition and multiple voices. High values =                             party respects democratic norms. 

6. `respect_opponents` - Captures whether a party treats rivals as legitimate or demonizes them.                         
7.  `minority_rights` -Reflects commitment to protecting ethnic/religious/linguistic minorities.                        
8.  `gender_equality` - Reflects support for gender equality in rights and representation.
